Strawberry Cream Cake

A fruity dessert, with alternating layers of fluffy and sponge cake, strawberries, and whipped cream.

Ingredients:

Sponge Cake-

  • 6 Whole eggs
  • 120g Powdered sugar
  • 120g All poupose flour

Whipped cream-

  • 250mL Cold whipping cream
  • 3tsp Granulated Sugar
  • 2tsp Vanilla extract
  • 125g Sliced strawberries
  • Whole strawberries(for garnishing)

Procedure:

  • Step 1- Carefully separate the egg-yolks from the whites and place the egg-whites into a large bowl
  • Step 2- Whip the egg-whites with an electric mixer while gradually adding the powdered sugar(about 2 tbsp at a time) until you obtain stiff peaks
  • Step 3- Whisk together the egg-yolks until they turn a pale yellow color, and fold that into the whipped egg-whites
  • Step 4- Gradually sieve the flour into the above mixture, folding it in gently until just combined. Do not overmix.
  • Step 5- Pour the batter equally into three identical circular cake pans
  • Step 6- Bake at 160°C for 30-35 minutes.
  • Step 7- Let the cakes cool off after they are done baking
  • Step 8- Now, into a separate bowl, pour the cold whipping cream
  • Step 9- Whip the cream with an electric mixture while gradually adding in the granulated sugar, until you obtain stiff peaks
  • Step 11- Add the vanilla extract and whip one last time
  • Step 12- Onto a cake stand, place one of the sponge cakes. Spread a generous later of whipped cream on the entire surface, and cover with sliced strawberries. Similarly alternate between these layers for the remaining cakes
  • Step 13- cover the outer surface of the cake with whipped cream and garnish with more sliced and whole strawberries and refrigerate
  • Step 14- Serve cold.

Enjoy~
